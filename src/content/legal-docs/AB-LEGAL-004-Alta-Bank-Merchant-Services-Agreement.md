## 1. Overview

This Merchant Services Agreement (“Agreement”) governs merchant services offered by Alta Bank.

Merchant services may include merchant accounts, Alta Pay, invoices, payment links, recurring invoices, branded payment pages, customer checkout pages, merchant analytics, Alta Commercial, payment notifications, payment records, and related tools.

By applying for, accessing, or using Alta Bank merchant services, the merchant agrees to this Agreement, the Alta Pay Terms, the Alta Bank Business Banking Agreement, the Alta Bank Deposit Account Agreement where applicable, the Alta Group Terms of Service, the Alta Group Privacy Policy, any applicable fee schedule, and any other rules or disclosures provided by Alta Bank.

Alta Bank may update this Agreement at any time.
