## 1. Overview

This Fee Schedule lists the standard fees charged by Alta Exchange for trading, listings, Alta Terminal services, market data, APIs, and related Alta Markets services.

Alta Exchange operates the exchange, listing system, trading infrastructure, market data services, and APIs. Alta Terminal is the customer-facing trading and portfolio platform operated by Alta Exchange. Alta Markets is the public-facing brand for Alta Exchange, Alta Terminal, market data, APIs, and related services.

If a fee is not listed in this Fee Schedule or another written Alta Exchange agreement, then the fee is currently not charged.

Alta Exchange may update this Fee Schedule at any time.
