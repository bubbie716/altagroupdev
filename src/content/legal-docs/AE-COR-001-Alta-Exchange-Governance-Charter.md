# AE-COR-001 — Alta Exchange Governance Charter

**Entity:** Alta Exchange  
**Document ID:** AE-COR-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active upon approval  
**Approved By:** Alta Group N.V.  
**Effective Date:** Immediately upon approval  
**Last Updated:** July 6, 2026  

---

## 1. Purpose

Alta Exchange is a wholly owned subsidiary of Alta Group N.V. responsible for operating the Alta market platform.

Alta Exchange may operate services including:

- company listings;
- IPOs and public offerings;
- trading infrastructure;
- brokerage-style user access;
- market data;
- exchange APIs;
- issuer tools;
- investor tools; and
- Alta Terminal.

Alta Exchange exists to provide a structured marketplace for companies, investors, and financial activity within the Alta ecosystem.

---

## 2. Ownership

Alta Exchange is owned and controlled by Alta Group N.V.

No person or entity shall obtain ownership, control, voting power, or management authority over Alta Exchange unless approved by Alta Group.

Alta Group may reorganize, merge, rename, expand, restrict, or dissolve Alta Exchange at its discretion.

---

## 3. Relationship with Alta Group

Alta Exchange operates under the authority of Alta Group.

Alta Group retains final authority over:

- the appointment or removal of Alta Exchange leadership;
- major business decisions;
- listing standards;
- exchange rules;
- major technology changes;
- fees and revenue models;
- capital allocation;
- debt or financing;
- mergers, acquisitions, or reorganizations;
- legal documents;
- use of Alta trademarks and branding; and
- any matter reserved to Alta Group under its corporate documents.

Alta Exchange may conduct normal daily operations without separate approval unless the matter is reserved to Alta Group.

---

## 4. Management

Alta Exchange shall be managed by a Director appointed by the Chairman of Alta Group.

The Director is responsible for daily operations, including exchange administration, product management, listing review, staff supervision, and implementation of Alta Group directives.

The Chairman of Alta Group may remove or replace the Director at any time.

The Director may appoint managers, employees, contractors, moderators, or agents as needed to support Alta Exchange operations, subject to Alta Group authority.

---

## 5. Alta Terminal

Alta Terminal is a product and business line of Alta Exchange.

Alta Terminal is not a separate subsidiary unless Alta Group later decides otherwise.

Alta Terminal may provide users with access to:

- account dashboards;
- portfolio tools;
- watchlists;
- market data;
- company profiles;
- trading tools;
- order entry;
- research;
- issuer information; and
- other market-related tools.

Alta Exchange shall be responsible for operating, maintaining, and governing Alta Terminal.

---

## 6. Authority of Alta Exchange

Subject to Alta Group oversight, Alta Exchange may:

- operate an exchange platform;
- approve, reject, suspend, or delist companies;
- publish listing standards;
- publish trading rules;
- maintain issuer profiles;
- provide market data;
- manage IPO workflows;
- operate Alta Terminal;
- operate exchange APIs;
- charge fees;
- create internal operating procedures;
- communicate with issuers, users, and investors;
- enforce exchange rules; and
- take actions reasonably necessary to operate the marketplace.

---

## 7. Limitations

Alta Exchange may not do any of the following without Alta Group approval:

- issue equity or ownership interests;
- create a subsidiary;
- borrow money;
- approve major expenses outside approved budgets;
- sell or transfer major assets;
- transfer intellectual property;
- materially change its business model;
- enter into major partnerships;
- waive important legal rights;
- change its name or branding;
- amend this Charter;
- dissolve, merge, or reorganize; or
- take any action reserved to Alta Group.

---

## 8. Listings and Issuers

Alta Exchange may establish requirements for companies seeking to list on the exchange.

Alta Exchange may approve, deny, suspend, or remove listed companies based on its listing rules, issuer conduct, disclosure quality, fraud risk, inactivity, or other marketplace concerns.

Listing on Alta Exchange does not create ownership in Alta Exchange or Alta Group.

---

## 9. Trading and Market Rules

Alta Exchange may create and enforce trading rules for users, issuers, and market participants.

These rules may address:

- trading access;
- order types;
- market manipulation;
- insider activity;
- issuer disclosures;
- trading suspensions;
- account restrictions;
- user conduct;
- fees;
- API access; and
- dispute procedures.

Alta Exchange may suspend or restrict trading when necessary to protect marketplace integrity.

---

## 10. Financial Reporting

Alta Exchange shall maintain records of its revenue, expenses, assets, liabilities, and other financial activity.

Alta Exchange shall provide financial information to Alta Group upon request.

Alta Group may consolidate Alta Exchange financial activity into Alta Group financial statements.

---

## 11. Technology and Intellectual Property

All software, websites, APIs, bots, code, databases, trademarks, trade names, domains, designs, documents, and other intellectual property used by Alta Exchange are owned by Alta Group unless Alta Group states otherwise in writing.

Alta Exchange receives an internal license to use Alta Group technology and intellectual property for its approved business operations.

Alta Exchange may not sell, transfer, license, copy, or assign Alta Group intellectual property without Alta Group approval.

---

## 12. Branding

Alta Exchange may use the Alta name, Alta Exchange name, Alta Terminal name, logos, designs, and related branding only with permission from Alta Group.

Alta Group may update, restrict, or revoke branding rules at any time.

---

## 13. Compliance with Alta Group Documents

Alta Exchange shall operate consistently with:

- Alta Group Articles of Incorporation;
- Alta Group Bylaws;
- Alta Group Shareholders' Agreement;
- Alta Group Reserved Powers Resolution;
- this Charter;
- Alta Exchange customer agreements;
- Alta Exchange listing rules;
- Alta Exchange trading rules; and
- any other applicable Alta Group or Alta Exchange documents.

If there is a conflict, Alta Group documents control unless Alta Group decides otherwise.

---

## 14. Amendments

Alta Group may amend, replace, suspend, or terminate this Charter at any time.

No amendment shall be valid unless approved by Alta Group.

---

## 15. Approval

This Charter is adopted by Alta Group as the governing document for Alta Exchange.
