## 1. Overview

This Alta Terminal Customer Agreement (“Agreement”) governs access to and use of Alta Terminal.

Alta Terminal is a brokerage-style trading, portfolio, and market access platform operated by **Alta Terminal**, a product of Alta Group N.V.

By opening an Alta Terminal account, placing orders, viewing holdings, trading securities, using market tools, or accessing related services, the customer agrees to this Agreement, the Alta Group Terms of Service, the Alta Group Privacy Policy, any applicable Terminal fee schedule and other rules or disclosures provided by Alta Terminal or Alta Group.

Alta Terminal may update this Agreement at any time.

- Alta Terminal does not operate a securities exchange;
- Alta Terminal accounts are not real-world brokerage accounts;

- use of Alta Terminal does not create real-world investment, securities, banking, legal, or financial rights;

Customers are responsible for understanding that Alta Terminal is a roleplay and virtual economy product unless expressly stated otherwise.
