## 1. Overview

This Fee Schedule lists the standard fees charged by Newport Clearing Corporation (“NCC”) for participation, routing, clearing, settlement, technical access, and related infrastructure services.

NCC is the clearing, settlement, routing, and interbank infrastructure subsidiary of Alta Group N.V.

If a fee is not listed in this Fee Schedule or another written NCC agreement, then the fee is currently not charged.

NCC may update this Fee Schedule at any time.

---

## 2. Roleplay and Virtual Economy Notice

NCC is designed for use within Minecraft, Discord, roleplay communities, virtual economies, and related online environments.

Unless NCC clearly states otherwise in writing:

- fees are charged in the applicable in-game, fictional, simulated, or roleplay currency;
- fees do not represent real-world clearing house, central bank, payment system, settlement, routing, or bank network fees;
- NCC does not process real-world bank transfers;
- NCC does not operate a real-world clearing house;
- NCC services do not create real-world banking, payment, securities, investment, legal, or financial rights.

---

## 8. Custom and Special Fees

NCC may charge custom, manual, negotiated, or case-specific fees if disclosed to the institution before or at the time the service is provided.

Custom fees may apply to:

- dedicated infrastructure;
- custom routing integrations;
- custom settlement setup;
- high-volume processing;
- custom reporting;
- emergency manual work;
- complex investigations;
- special institution onboarding;
- enterprise support;
- large or unusual operations;
- products not listed in this Fee Schedule.

No custom fee applies unless NCC clearly states it.

## 10. Fee Changes

NCC may update this Fee Schedule at any time.

Updated fees become effective when posted, published, or otherwise made available, unless NCC states a later effective date.

Continued participation in NCC after a fee update means the participating institution accepts the updated fees.

---

## 11. Fee Collection

NCC may collect fees by:

- deducting from a settlement account;
- deducting from institution balances;
- deducting from reserves where allowed;
- issuing an invoice;
- requiring manual payment;
- charging an Alta Bank account where permitted;
- using another approved billing method.

NCC may restrict, suspend, downgrade, or terminate services for unpaid fees.

---

## 12. Refunds of Fees

Fees are generally non-refundable unless NCC decides otherwise or a specific written agreement states otherwise.

NCC may issue fee refunds, credits, reversals, or adjustments at its discretion.
