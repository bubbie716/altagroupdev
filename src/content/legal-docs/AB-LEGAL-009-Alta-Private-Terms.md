# AB-LEGAL-009 — Alta Private Terms

**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Overview

Alta Private is an invitation-based relationship tier offered by Alta Bank to selected customers. These Terms apply in addition to the Alta Bank Deposit Account Agreement, applicable card or loan agreements, the Alta Group Terms of Service, and published fee schedules.

## 2. Virtual-Economy Notice

Alta Private is part of Alta's Minecraft, Discord, roleplay, and virtual-economy services. It is not real-world private banking, wealth management, fiduciary service, investment advice, tax advice, or legal advice.

## 3. Eligibility and Invitation

Eligibility may consider account history, activity, relationship depth, platform conduct, product use, financial-style profile, staff review, and other internal factors.

An eligibility indication does not guarantee an invitation. An invitation is personal, non-transferable, and subject to acceptance before expiration.

## 4. Activation

Membership begins when Alta records acceptance and activates the customer. Alta may require updated account, security, or eligibility information before activation.

## 5. Benefits

Benefits may include:

- a designated relationship contact or enhanced support path;
- access to selected Alta Private pages, communications, or events;
- eligibility for designated card or banking products;
- relationship-based review of requests; and
- other benefits shown in the customer's dashboard or invitation.

Benefits are not guaranteed approvals, fixed service levels, investment recommendations, or preferential treatment in disputes, security investigations, or enforcement matters.

## 6. Product-Specific Terms

An Alta Private-branded card, account, loan, or other product remains governed by its own agreement and terms schedule. Alta Private membership alone does not create credit, increase limits, waive fees, or change an existing product.

## 7. Relationship Information

Alta may use account and relationship information to assess eligibility, assign service contacts, identify relevant products, and maintain the membership, as described in the Alta Group Privacy Policy.

## 8. Customer Responsibilities

Members must:

- keep account and contact information accurate;
- protect access credentials and private communications;
- communicate respectfully with staff;
- avoid misrepresenting membership or benefits;
- comply with all product agreements and platform rules; and
- promptly report suspected unauthorized access.

## 9. No Transfer or Public Representation

Membership, invitations, benefits, and private communications may not be sold, transferred, shared, or used to imply an ownership interest, employment relationship, regulatory status, or endorsement beyond the benefit Alta actually provides.

## 10. Changes to Benefits

Alta may add, modify, suspend, or remove benefits based on product availability, operational capacity, risk, or program design. Alta will not retroactively change a separately executed card or loan agreement through these Terms.

## 11. Suspension and Termination

Alta may suspend or end membership because of account closure, inactivity, loss of eligibility, misuse, security concerns, abusive conduct, unpaid obligations, or violation of Alta agreements.

A customer may decline an invitation or request removal from Alta Private. Ending membership does not automatically close the customer's underlying Alta Bank accounts or cancel obligations under another agreement.

## 12. Conflicts and Fair Treatment

Alta Private may provide differentiated service and product access, but does not override Alta's authority, conflict-of-interest controls, security requirements, or obligation to apply documented review procedures.

## 13. Availability and Liability

Relationship contacts, benefits, and private channels may be unavailable during maintenance, staff absence, platform outages, or program changes. Alta's liability remains subject to the applicable Alta Group and product terms.

## 14. Changes and Contact

Alta may update these Terms prospectively. Questions may be directed through the Alta Private contact shown in the customer's account or through official Alta support.
