# AT-LEGAL-003 — Alta Terminal Risk Disclosure

**Entity:** Alta Terminal LLC  
**Document ID:** AT-LEGAL-003  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active disclosure  
**Last Updated:** July 21, 2026  

---

## 1. Purpose

This disclosure summarizes material risks associated with Alta Terminal. It cannot identify every possible risk. Customers should use only virtual value they can afford to lose completely.

## 2. Virtual and Roleplay Value

Alta Terminal instruments, cash, returns, and positions are fictional, simulated, in-game, or roleplay records unless Alta Group expressly states otherwise. They may have no real-world monetary value, legal ownership status, redemption right, insurance, or government protection.

## 3. Pre-Launch and Feature Risk

Alta Terminal is under development. Features may be unavailable, incomplete, reset, materially changed, or never launched. Demonstrations and roadmap descriptions are not commitments.

## 4. Market and Price Risk

Prices may change rapidly. A position may lose some or all of its virtual value. Historical prices, simulated performance, rankings, and charts do not predict future results.

## 5. Liquidity Risk

There may be few or no willing counterparties. A Customer may be unable to enter or exit a position at the displayed price, in the requested quantity, or at all.

## 6. Order Risk

Orders may be delayed, rejected, partially filled, filled at different prices, remain open, or execute before a cancellation arrives. Different order types exchange price certainty, execution opportunity, and timing in different ways.

## 7. Third-Party Provider Risk

Alta Terminal may rely on independent providers for execution, venue access, custody records, market data, settlement, identity, banking, or communications. A provider may experience outages, change its rules, restrict an instrument, reject an instruction, provide incorrect data, suspend service, or cease operating.

Alta Terminal cannot guarantee a provider’s solvency, governance, fairness, security, or continued availability.

## 8. Custody and Record Risk

Positions and balances may depend on provider or platform records rather than possession of a transferable real-world asset. Records may require reconciliation and may be frozen while discrepancies are investigated.

## 9. Settlement and Counterparty Risk

A completed execution may still fail to settle. A provider, issuer, counterparty, payment source, or settlement service may fail to deliver required virtual cash or assets. Corrections or reversals may occur after an earlier provisional display.

## 10. Issuer Risk

An issuer may provide inaccurate information, become inactive, act fraudulently, fail to honor a corporate action, lose community standing, or cease operating. Alta Terminal does not guarantee issuer conduct or disclosures.

## 11. Market Data Risk

Quotes, charts, news, reference data, and analytics may be delayed, stale, incomplete, duplicated, corrected, or unavailable. A displayed price may not be executable.

## 12. Technology and Cybersecurity Risk

Internet failures, software defects, credential compromise, unauthorized access, dependency failures, malicious activity, data corruption, deployment mistakes, or hardware outages may affect access or records.

## 13. Community and Platform Risk

Minecraft servers, Discord communities, roleplay governments, rules, ownership, and player populations may change abruptly. Community conflict, server closure, migration, or administrative action may impair or eliminate virtual value and services.

## 14. Concentration Risk

Holding a large portion of virtual value in one instrument, issuer, provider, industry, account, or community increases exposure to a single failure.

## 15. Corporate-Action Risk

Splits, distributions, mergers, symbol changes, cancellations, and other actions may be delayed, disputed, corrected, or inconsistently implemented by issuers and providers.

## 16. Operational-Control Risk

Risk limits, account restrictions, trading interruptions, provider controls, or emergency procedures may prevent a Customer from acting when desired. Controls may be introduced or changed in response to new threats.

## 17. No Advice

Alta Terminal does not evaluate whether a transaction is suitable for a Customer and does not provide personalized investment advice. Research, rankings, analytics, and educational content are informational or roleplay tools.

## 18. No Real-World Protection

Alta Terminal is not a real-world regulated brokerage, bank, exchange, custodian, or clearing agency. Real-world investor compensation, deposit insurance, securities regulation, and similar protections do not apply merely because brokerage terminology is used.

## 19. Total-Loss Acknowledgment

By using an enabled trading or position feature, the Customer acknowledges the possibility of losing the entire virtual value of an instrument or account and accepts the risks described here and in the Customer Agreement.
