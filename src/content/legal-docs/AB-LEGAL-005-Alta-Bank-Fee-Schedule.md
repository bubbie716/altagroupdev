## 1. Overview

This Fee Schedule lists the standard fees charged by Alta Bank for accounts, payments, merchant services, subscriptions, and related products.

If a fee is not listed in this Fee Schedule or another written Alta Bank agreement, then the fee is currently not charged.

Alta Bank may update this Fee Schedule at any time.

---

## 2. Roleplay and Virtual Economy Notice

Alta Bank is designed for use within Minecraft, Discord, roleplay communities, virtual economies, and related online environments.

Unless Alta Bank clearly states otherwise in writing:

- fees are charged in the applicable in-game, fictional, simulated, or roleplay currency;
- fees do not represent real-world banking fees;
- Alta Bank does not process real-world money;
- Alta Bank does not hold real-world deposits;
- Alta Bank services do not create real-world banking, payment, investment, or legal rights.

## 5. Alta Commercial Pro

Alta Commercial Pro is the paid premium business and merchant services tier offered by Alta Bank.

Unless changed by Alta Bank, the standard Alta Commercial Pro fee is:

> **ƒ10,000 per month**

Alta Commercial Pro may include premium features such as:

- higher merchant limits;
- unlimited or expanded invoices;
- unlimited or expanded payment links;
- recurring invoices;
- branded invoices;
- branded payment pages;
- advanced merchant analytics;
- expanded team permissions;
- priority support;
- other premium commercial tools approved by Alta Bank.

Alta Bank may add, remove, limit, or modify Alta Commercial Pro features at any time.

---

## 6. Billing

Alta Commercial Pro fees may be charged monthly to the business account selected by the business.

The business is responsible for maintaining sufficient funds to pay subscription fees.

If the selected account has insufficient funds, Alta Bank may:

- retry billing;
- notify the business;
- place the subscription in a grace period;
- downgrade the business to Alta Commercial Core;
- restrict premium features;
- pause recurring invoices;
- suspend branded pages;
- collect unpaid fees from available balances where allowed by Alta Bank rules.

## 8. Special, Manual, or Custom Fees

Alta Bank may charge special, manual, custom, negotiated, or case-specific fees if disclosed to the customer, business, merchant, or institution before or at the time the service is provided.

These may include fees for:

- custom business services;
- custom merchant setups;
- premium support;
- manual administrative work;
- special account reviews;
- custom integrations;
- enterprise services;
- large or unusual operations;

## 9. Fee Changes

Alta Bank may update this Fee Schedule at any time.

Updated fees become effective when posted, published, or otherwise made available, unless Alta Bank states a later effective date.

Continued use of Alta Bank services after a fee update means the user, business, or merchant accepts the updated fees.

## 10. Fee Collection

Alta Bank may collect fees by:

- deducting from an Alta Bank account;
- deducting from a business account;
- deducting from merchant payment proceeds;
- deducting from available balances;
- issuing an invoice;
- requiring manual payment;
- using another approved billing method.

Alta Bank may restrict, suspend, downgrade, or terminate services for unpaid fees.

---

## 11. Refunds of Fees

Fees are generally non-refundable unless Alta Bank decides otherwise or a specific written agreement states otherwise.

Alta Bank may issue fee refunds, credits, reversals, or adjustments at its discretion.
