## 1. Overview

This Business Banking Agreement (“Agreement”) governs business bank accounts and related business banking services offered by Alta Bank.

By opening, maintaining, accessing, or using an Alta Bank business account, the business and its authorized users agree to this Agreement, the Alta Bank Deposit Account Agreement where applicable, the Alta Group Terms of Service, the Alta Group Privacy Policy, any applicable fee schedule, and any other rules or disclosures provided by Alta Bank.

Alta Bank may update this Agreement at any time.

---

## 2. Roleplay and Virtual Economy Notice

Alta Bank is designed for use within Minecraft, Discord, roleplay communities, virtual economies, and related online environments.

Unless Alta Bank clearly states otherwise in writing:

- Alta Bank is not a real-world bank;
- Alta Bank does not hold real-world business deposits;
- Alta Bank business accounts are not real-world bank accounts;
- balances may represent in-game, fictional, simulated, or roleplay value only;
- business accounts may depend on the rules of the relevant Minecraft server, Discord server, or roleplay community;
- Alta Bank services do not create real-world banking, investment, legal, or corporate rights.

Businesses and authorized users are responsible for understanding that Alta Bank is a roleplay and virtual economy service unless expressly stated otherwise.

## 3. Eligible Businesses

Alta Bank may allow companies, organizations, merchants, institutions, or other approved entities to open business accounts.

Alta Bank may require a business to:

- submit a business account application;
- identify its owner or authorized representative;
- provide accurate company information;
- connect or identify a Discord server, Discord account, or Minecraft identity;
- complete verification steps;
- maintain accurate records;
- agree to this Agreement;
- follow Alta Bank and Alta Group rules.

Alta Bank may approve, deny, suspend, restrict, or close a business account at its discretion.

## 4. Business Account Ownership

A business account belongs to the approved business or organization within the Alta Bank system.

The business owner, founder, executive, or approved representative is responsible for the account unless Alta Bank records show otherwise.

Alta Bank may rely on its records to determine:

- who owns or controls the business account;
- who is authorized to act for the business;
- which users have account permissions;
- which users may approve transactions;
- which users may manage account settings.

Use of a business account does not create ownership in Alta Bank, Alta Group, or any Alta subsidiary.
