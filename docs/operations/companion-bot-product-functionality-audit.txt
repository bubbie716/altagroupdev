Alta Companion Discord Bot — Product Functionality Audit
=========================================================
Lens: V1 launch readiness for a lightweight banking app inside Discord
Not in scope: Code quality, embed redesign
Sources: altabot/ hub, commands, deal-room bridge, plus altaweb backend contracts
Date: July 3, 2026


Overall Launch Readiness Score: 7.2 / 10
-----------------------------------------
Ship as a companion with a short pre-launch fix list — not as primary banking.
Core read/write banking is real and boundaries are strong. Safety and polish gaps
(especially Alta Pay) keep it from feeling finished.

------------------------  ------  ------------------------------------------

================================================================================
1. TOP STRENGTHS
================================================================================

  1. Real banking in Discord — deposit (with proof), withdraw, transfer, Alta Pay
     are implemented, not link wrappers
  2. Correct product boundary — lending, card apps, admin, exchange, company mgmt
     stay on web
  3. Mobile-app navigation — persistent Home / Accounts / Deposit / Withdraw /
     Transfer + Alta Pay row feels familiar
  4. Transfer confirmation with fees — bank-grade disclosure before money moves
     between own accounts
  5. Alta Pay breadth for V1 — people, verified companies, bank accounts, Alta Card
     funding (with employer guardrails)
  6. Personal vs business accounts — separate sections, labels, treasury-aware Alta Pay
  7. Deal Rooms as private channels — better than DMs for staff/customer threads;
     bidirectional sync with website as record
  8. Alta Private invitation DMs — ceremonial accept/decline; conditional nav feels
     earned
  9. Discord-specific limits + web escalation — e.g. large transfers hand off to web
 10. Push notification pipeline — scales to Exchange, Terminal, NCC without
     rebuilding bot UX


================================================================================
2. TOP WEAKNESSES
================================================================================

  1. Alta Pay has no confirmation step — Transfer confirms; Alta Pay sends
     immediately after amount entry
  2. Notifications "read" gap — no hub inbox/history; only outbound DMs
  3. No smart defaults — website Alta Pay funding default ignored; every action
     re-picks accounts
  4. Alta Pay UX fragmentation — recipient search opens separate ephemeral message
  5. Home is passive — balances and counts, but no pending detail, quick actions,
     or Transfer vs Pay explainer
  6. Pending requests opaque — deposit/withdraw counts only, no ref/amount/status
  7. Alta Pay limits not surfaced — unlike Transfer, no visible limits in bot
  8. Session expiry mid-flow — "Run /bank again" frustrating during multi-step pay
  9. Alta Private member hub is a signpost — status + web link only
 10. Inconsistent safety UX — Transfer protected; Alta Pay and large withdrawals less

================================================================================
SPECIFIC QUESTIONS — DIRECT ANSWERS
================================================================================

Is /bank too crowded?
  No. Row 1 is at Discord's 5-button max and maps to a mobile tab bar. Row 2
  (Alta Pay, optional Alta Private, Open Website) is appropriate. Crowding is
  in Alta Pay sub-flows, not top-level nav.

Are there too many buttons?
  Top level: no. Per-screen: sometimes — Transfer confirm adds 2; Accounts
  pagination adds up to 4. Acceptable for power users with many accounts.

Should any features be hidden unless relevant?
  Yes, recommended:
    - Alta Private nav — members + active invitees only; hide eligible tease
    - Alta Pay card funding — only if user has Alta Card
    - Business sections — hide when zero business accounts
    - Transfer — hide or explain when only one account

Are Deposit, Withdraw, Transfer, and Alta Pay clear?
  Mostly yes in copy ("your own accounts" vs "another customer or company").
  Behavior undermines clarity: Transfer confirms; Alta Pay doesn't; search
  breaks out of hub. Fix: confirmation + Home explainer + inline search.

Are company accounts easy to understand inside Accounts?
  Yes structurally — separate Personal/Business, Account · Company labels,
  company field on detail. Could improve with business badge and "treasury
  account" wording.

Should users have default accounts?
  For Alta Pay funding: yes — honor website defaultAltaPayFundingAccountId.
  For deposit/withdraw/transfer: V2 — last-used memory nice but not V1-critical.

Should there be quick actions on account pages?
  Yes — V1 nice-to-have, high value. Deposit/Withdraw/Transfer/Pay from this
  account would materially improve speed.

Should Alta Pay support people and companies in V1?
  Yes — keep both. Already implemented with sensible rules (person = bank-funded;
  card = companies only, employer excluded). Differentiator; don't cut it.

Are Deal Rooms better as private channels than DMs?
  Yes. Multi-party, persistent, staff-visible. Website sync preserves official
  record. Correct V1 architecture.

Are any website-only features missing from Discord?
  Intentionally missing (correct): lending apps, card apps, statements,
  scheduled transfers, settings, full history.

  Gaps customers may notice:
    - Pending request detail
    - Notification history / settings link
    - Alta Pay confirmation parity
    - Default funding account
    - Staff Secure Deal Room message alerts (in-app on web; DM gap)

Are any Discord features risky and should move to website?
  Alta Pay without confirmation     Fix in bot, don't remove
  Large withdrawals without confirm  Consider confirm above threshold
  Deposit proof in Discord           Keep — native advantage
  Card-funded Alta Pay to companies  Keep with confirmation + copy
  Deal Room chat in Discord          Keep — channel model is right
  Server Box auto-credit             Keep disabled / avoid

stay outbound, never a full inbox.
